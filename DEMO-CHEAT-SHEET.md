# VECTOR DEMO CHEAT SHEET (10 min)

## 1. OPENING (1 min)
**Say:** "Vector solves 3 problems:"
- **Fragmentation** → OKRs in one place, not scattered
- **Misalignment** → Areas cascade from company goals
- **Blind spots** → Real-time visibility, not quarterly surprises

---

## 2. HOME PAGE (1 min)
| Stat | Say This |
|------|----------|
| **5 Global OKRs** | "Our strategic bets - focused, not 50 priorities" |
| **37 Key Results** | "Measurable outcomes, not activities" |
| **14% On Track** | "We know this NOW, not at year-end" |

**Power phrase:** *"Your morning coffee view"*

---

## 3. 2026 OKRs PAGE (4 min)

### Global OKRs
- Point to OKR-1 through OKR-5
- **Say:** "Each has ONE owner (initials on right)"
- Expand OKR-5 → Show Key Results

### Key Results
- **Say:** "Not 'are we working on it?' but 'are we hitting the target?'"
- Separation milestones: 0% → 100%
- Cost synergies: 10% → 40%

### Area OKRs (nested below)
- Point to GCC India, GCC Mexico, GPL, GBS Onshore
- **Say:** "Each area asked: What must WE deliver?"
- Hover on **Quality 0/8** → "8 criteria for well-defined OKRs"

**Power phrase:** *"No more asking 'send me your OKRs'"*

---

## 4. OKR TRACKING PAGE (3 min)

### Top Section
- **5 tabs** = 5 Global OKRs (orange = selected)
- **Filters** = Focus by Area or Owner
- **Say:** "Filter and ask: 'Walk me through your reds'"

### Progress Cards - THE MONEY SHOT

| Color | Status | Say This |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Green | On track (83%) | "No action needed" |
| Orange | Progressing (70%) | "Monitor - what's blocking?" |
| Red | Off track (13%) | "Intervene NOW" |
| Gray | No check-in | "Accountability gap - flag it" |

**Power phrase:** *"This is where you spend your time"* (point to red)

### Check-in Buttons
- **Say:** "Teams update here. No spreadsheets, no waiting."

---

## 5. CLOSE (1 min)

**For You (Head of Org):**
- Strategic clarity - 5 priorities, not 50
- Real-time visibility - problems surface early
- Accountability - owners + statuses visible

**For Areas:**
- Clear line from their work → company strategy
- Show progress without slide decks

**Say:** "Dashboards coming soon. Questions?"
